I am a Researcher at Nokia Bell Labs, Stuttgart. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems where I was advised by Krishna P. Gummadi and Alan Mislove.

I am broadly interested in systems security, privacy and data mining topics.
